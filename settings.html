Get your account token:

  1. Access the InApplet website and click Login
  2. Log in to your account or create your account
  3. On the right side of the menu choose a project or create a new one
  4. After creating the project, click on the menu under Settings
  5. The list of API Tokens will be displayed on the settings screen, copy one of the tokens and insert in the Token fields in the form below
  6. The slug is in the tracking code next to window.account_slug
  7. Okay, now your account is set up, the accesses you make on the TrackView will be displayed on the TrackView itself as a demonstration
